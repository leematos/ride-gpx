// What's inside

The whole trainer app — in a browser tab.

Everything you need to ride real-world routes indoors. Nothing you don't.

01 · TERRAIN

Photorealistic 3D terrain

Every route rendered on Google Photorealistic 3D Maps with a follow camera, rider beacon, minimap, and cinematic overviews — orbit, ellipse fly-by, figure-eight fly-over, or straight-down satellite.

Orbit Fly-by Fly-over Satellite
02 · TRAINER

Real grade, real resistance

Connect a Wahoo KICKR or any FTMS trainer over Web Bluetooth. Pedal and the map moves at your speed; the road grade drives your resistance live.

03 · SENSORS

Heart-rate & power zones

Pair a Bluetooth strap for live BPM. HR and power zones computed from your resting/max HR and FTP.

04 · ROUTE IQ

Climb detection

Auto-classifies difficulty and finds every sustained climb — length, gain, grade, and live progress to the top.

05 · EXPORT

.FIT export

Rides record locally and download as virtual-ride .fit files for Strava, Garmin Connect, or intervals.icu.
